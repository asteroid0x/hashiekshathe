Digital Records

Transparency & Accountability: Access Our Digital Records

At Hashi Ekshathe, we believe in complete transparency when it comes to how donations are collected and used. That’s why we maintain publicly accessible digital records powered by Google Sheets, ensuring that every contribution is accounted for.

Donation Log

Every donation we receive is logged in real time, providing a clear and detailed breakdown of contributions. You can see who donated, when, and how their generosity is helping us move forward in our mission.

Expenditure Log

We ensure that every taka is spent wisely and effectively. Our expenditure log tracks where funds are allocated—from direct aid and community projects to operational costs—so you can see exactly how your donations are making an impact.

View Our Records Anytime

We believe trust is built on accountability. Our records are always up to date and accessible, allowing you to see the impact of your support firsthand. Together, we can create meaningful change with honesty, integrity, and transparency.
